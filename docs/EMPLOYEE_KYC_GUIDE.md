# Employee KYC Submission Guide
## Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

---

## 📱 How to Submit Your KYC Documents via WhatsApp

### Step 1: Join the WhatsApp Service

**First Time Only:**
1. Save this number in your phone: **+1 415 523 8886**
2. Open WhatsApp
3. Send this message to +1 415 523 8886: `join [your-code]`
   - (Your HR will provide the join code)
4. Wait for confirmation message

---

### Step 2: Send Your Documents

You need to submit **3 documents** in total. You can send them in **any order**:

#### 📄 Required Documents:

1. **Aadhaar Card** (Front/Back)
   - Clear photo of your Aadhaar card
   - All text must be readable
   - No shadows or glare

2. **PAN Card**
   - Clear photo of your PAN card
   - All details visible
   - Good lighting

3. **Bank Document** (Any ONE of these)
   - Bank passbook (first page)
   - Bank statement (recent)
   - Cancelled cheque

---

### Step 3: How to Send

**For Each Document:**

1. Open WhatsApp chat with **+1 415 523 8886**

2. **Tap the 📎 (attachment) icon**

3. **Select "Camera" or "Gallery"**
   - **Camera**: Take a fresh photo right now
   - **Gallery**: Choose existing photo

4. **Send the photo**

5. **Wait for confirmation** (5-10 seconds)
   - ✅ Success: "Your KYC documents have been received (ID: xxxxx)"
   - ❌ Error: "Error processing your document. Please try again."

6. **If error**: Take a clearer photo and send again

7. **Repeat for remaining documents**

## 📸 Photo Guidelines

### ✅ DO:
- Use good lighting (natural daylight is best)
- Keep camera steady
- Capture entire document in frame
- Ensure all text is sharp and readable
- Use a plain background (dark surface works best)
- Take photo straight-on (not at an angle)

### ❌ DON'T:
- Use flash (causes glare)
- Take blurry or shaky photos
- Cut off any part of the document
- Send screenshots (send actual photos)
- Cover any information with your fingers
- Take photos in dim lighting

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Error processing your document"

**Possible reasons:**
1. **Photo is blurry** → Take a clearer photo
2. **Poor lighting** → Use better lighting
3. **Document not fully visible** → Capture entire document
4. **Wrong document type** → Make sure it's Aadhaar/PAN/Bank document

**Solution:**
- Take a new photo with better quality
- Ensure good lighting
- Send again

### "No response after sending photo"

**Wait 10-15 seconds**. AI processing takes time.

If still no response:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Resend the photo
3. Contact HR if problem persists

### "Which order should I send documents?"

**Any order is fine!** The system accepts documents in any sequence.
