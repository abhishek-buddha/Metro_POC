# KYC Submission via WhatsApp - Quick Guide
## One-Page SOP for Employees

---

## 📱 WhatsApp Number: **+1 415 523 8886**

---

## 🎯 3 Simple Steps

### Step 1: Join (First Time Only)
Send this message: **`join [your-code]`**
(HR will provide your code)

### Step 2: Send 3 Documents
Take clear photos and send them **one by one**:

| Document | What to Send |
|----------|--------------|
| **1. Aadhaar Card** | Front or back (clear photo) |
| **2. PAN Card** | Full card visible |
| **3. Bank Document** | Passbook first page OR Bank statement OR Cancelled cheque |

### Step 3: Wait for Confirmation
After each photo:
- ✅ Success: Save the ID number
- ❌ Error: Take clearer photo and resend

---

## 📸 Photo Tips

✅ **DO:**
- Use good lighting
- Capture full document
- Keep camera steady
- Plain dark background

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use flash
- Take blurry photos
- Cut off edges
- Use screenshots

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- Documents can be sent in **any order**
- Wait **5-10 seconds** between photos
- **Save your Submission ID** from confirmation message
- HR will review within **24-48 hours**

---

## 🔒 Your Data is Safe

- Aadhaar encrypted (only last 4 digits stored)
- PAN number encrypted
- Bank account encrypted
- WhatsApp end-to-end encrypted

---

## 🆘 Problems?

**Error message?** → Take clearer photo, send again

**No response?** → Wait 15 seconds, check internet, resend

**Still issues?** → Contact HR with your phone number

---

## ✅ Checklist Before Sending

- [ ] Joined WhatsApp number
- [ ] Photo is clear and sharp
- [ ] All text readable
- [ ] Full document visible
- [ ] Good lighting
